Probability Experiment Lab

Objective: Test whether experimental probability gets closer to theoretical probability as the number of trials increases.

45-Minute Lab Plan: Predict → Calculate theoretical probability → Run small trials → Run larger trials → Compare → Explain.

Teacher setup: Students move screen-by-screen. They cannot continue until each required section is completed. Students calculate probabilities themselves; no answer button is included.
